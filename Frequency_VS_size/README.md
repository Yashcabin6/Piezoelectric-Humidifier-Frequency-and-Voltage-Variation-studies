# Frequency vs Mean Bubble Diameter at Rated Voltage

This plot compares the mean bubble diameter obtained at three operating frequencies: 106 kHz, 108 kHz, and 110 kHz. For each frequency, the mean bubble diameter was calculated from the final filtered particle measurements, and the error bars represent one standard deviation of the measured bubble diameter distribution.

| Frequency (kHz) | Bubble Count | Mean Diameter (µm) | Standard Deviation (µm) | Minimum Diameter (µm) | Maximum Diameter (µm) |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 106 | 122 | 10.676 | 3.058 | 4.294 | 19.454 |
| 108 | 149 | 10.798 | 3.032 | 5.275 | 19.431 |
| 110 | 237 | 10.589 | 2.590 | 4.642 | 19.201 |
