# Voltage vs Mean Bubble Diameter

This plot compares the mean bubble diameter measured at three operating voltages: 3.7 V, 4.5 V, and 5.0 V. For each voltage, the mean bubble diameter was calculated from the final filtered particle measurements, and the error bars represent one standard deviation of the measured bubble diameter distribution.

| Voltage (V) | Bubble Count | Mean Diameter (µm) | Standard Deviation (µm) | Minimum Diameter (µm) | Maximum Diameter (µm) |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 3.7 | 220 | 10.701 | 3.749 | 4.435 | 21.573 |
| 4.5 | 190 | 10.782 | 3.407 | 4.089 | 19.593 |
| 5.0 | 149 | 10.798 | 3.032 | 5.275 | 19.431 |
